## Before We Start — 3 Quick Decisions

1. **ORM choice** — Prisma (schema-first, great DX) vs raw `pg` queries (more control). I recommend **Prisma** for this project.
2. **Monorepo tool** — Simple folders (`client/` + `server/`) vs Turborepo. I recommend **simple folders** at your scale.
3. **File storage** — Local disk for now, S3 later? Or start with **Cloudinary** (free tier, zero config)?
