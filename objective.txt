Prompt: School Cafeteria Food Ordering & Smart Payment System
Design and develop a modern web and mobile application for a school cafeteria food ordering and payment system.
Project Overview
The application will be used by schools, cafeteria vendors, parents, students, and administrators. The primary objective is to allow parents to pre-order meals for their children and manage cafeteria payments digitally.
User Roles
1. Parent

• Login using student credentials or linked parent account.
• View weekly/monthly cafeteria menu.
• Order breakfast, lunch, snacks, and beverages for their child.
• Schedule meals in advance (daily, weekly, or monthly).
• Cancel or modify orders before a specified cutoff time.
• View order history.
• Receive notifications for successful orders, cancellations, and low wallet balance.
• Recharge cafeteria wallet/card online.
• Monitor food purchases made by their child.
2. Student

• Use a digital cafeteria card (QR Code/NFC/RFID).
• Purchase food using wallet balance.
• View available balance.
• View transaction history.
• Scan QR code at cafeteria counter for payment.
3. Cafeteria Vendor

• Manage food menu.
• Add/Edit/Delete food items.
• Configure meal categories:
   * Breakfast
   * Lunch
   * Snacks
   * Beverages
• Define availability timings.
• Set daily quantity limits.
• View upcoming orders.
• Generate preparation reports.
• Manage inventory.
4. School Administrator

• Manage students and parents.
• Manage cafeteria vendors.
• Approve menu changes.
• View revenue reports.
• View student consumption reports.
• Manage wallet/card system.
• Configure school-specific rules.
Core Features
Food Ordering System

• Daily menu display.
• Weekly meal planner.
• Advance ordering.
• Meal subscription plans.
• Special diet options:
   * Vegetarian
   * Vegan
   * Gluten-Free
   * Sports Nutrition Meals
   * Allergy-specific meals
Wallet/Card System

• Every student receives a virtual cafeteria card.
• Parents can recharge the card online.
• Wallet transactions are recorded.
• Real-time balance updates.
• Auto recharge option.
• Refund management.
Payment Integration
Support:

• UPI
• Credit/Debit Cards
• Net Banking
• Digital Wallets
QR/RFID Payment

• Generate unique QR code for each student.
• Optional RFID smart card integration.
• Fast payment at cafeteria counter.
Notifications

• Order confirmation.
• Meal reminders.
• Low wallet balance alerts.
• Recharge confirmation.
• Monthly expenditure reports.
Analytics Dashboard
Parent Dashboard

• Spending analysis.
• Favorite foods.
• Nutrition summary.
School Dashboard

• Daily sales.
• Most ordered meals.
• Peak ordering times.
• Vendor performance.
Vendor Dashboard

• Orders by meal type.
• Revenue reports.
• Inventory consumption.
Advanced Features
AI Recommendation Engine

• Suggest meals based on:
   * Previous orders
   * Nutrition goals
   * Student preferences
   * Sports activities
Nutrition Tracking
Display:

• Calories
• Protein
• Carbohydrates
• Fat
• Allergens
Sports Student Meal Plans

• High Protein Meals
• Athlete Meal Packages
• Custom Nutrition Plans
Attendance Integration

• Automatically cancel meals if student is absent.
• Refund wallet amount automatically.
Emergency Parent Control

• Instant card lock/unlock.
• Spending limits.
• Restricted food categories.
Expected Outcome
Build a scalable SaaS platform that enables schools to digitize cafeteria operations, allows parents to conveniently pre-order meals, provides cashless transactions through student cafeteria cards, tracks nutrition and spending, and improves cafeteria efficiency through analytics and automation.
Business Goal: Create a solution similar to a combination of a school ERP, food delivery app, and prepaid smart card system specifically designed for school cafeterias.
